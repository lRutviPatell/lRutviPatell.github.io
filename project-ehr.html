EHR Impact on Healthcare Quality

Academic case study on the role of electronic health records in improving care delivery.

Problem

Fragmented patient records and manual workflows reduce efficiency and increase clinical errors.

Approach

Reviewed literature, analyzed clinical workflows, and evaluated EHR-enabled data integration.

Outcome

Findings show improved data accuracy, faster decision-making, and enhanced patient safety.
